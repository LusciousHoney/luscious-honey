## The Foundation

**Take me back to the moment the House first became necessary to you. Where were you, and what was happening?**

THE MOMENT THAT I FIRST REALIZED THE LUSCIOUS HONEY COLLECTIVE WAS A THING WAS WHEN I CAST ALL OR MOST OF THE MAIN CHARACTERS AND PULL ME UNDER, AND EACH PERSON THAT VOICED AND BROUGHT EACH CHARACTER TO LIFE DURING THE TIKTOK LIVES, EACH HAD SPECIAL TALENTS THAT THEY BROUGHT TO THE TABLE EVEN EXPERIENCE LEVELS LIKE NONE OF THEM HAVE EVER DONE ANYTHING LIKE THIS BEFORE, BUT THEY WERE WILLING TO TRY AND EVEN WITH TK HAVING EITHER EXPERIENCE AND TK HAVING THEATER EXPERIENCE HIM HAVING A PLACE TO BE ABLE TO USE HIS VOICE IN ME PROVIDING THAT VOICE WAS VERY INTENTIONAL AND I'M TRYING TO FIND THE WORDS BECAUSE IT WAS A MATCH MADE IN HEAVEN. IT WAS LIKE TRULY DYNAMIC AND I ALL I PICKED EVERYONE I HAD SEPARATE MEETINGS AND CONNECTIONS WITH THEM AT DIFFERENT TIMES, AND WE FINALLY ALL BECAME A UNIT. IT WAS IT WAS ABSOLUTELY MAGICAL AND AFTER OUR SECOND EPISODE OF READING TOGETHER, I KNEW THAT WE NEEDED TO BE CALLED THE LUSCIOUS HONEY COLLECTIVE and from there it wasn't until weeks later when I kept running into other artist having interviews introducing music and seeing how many other artistic people I was coming across that I realize the collective wasn't just the four of us. It was more than that.

**What piece of work — a book, a song, a film, a conversation — set you on this path? Describe your first encounter with it.**

NOT SURE IF YOU MEAN ON THE PATH OF CREATIVE WRITING OF TURNING PULL ME UNDER INTO A FULL-FLEDGED NOVEL OR PUT ME ON THE PATH OF WANTING TO HAVE A PLACE WHERE WE ALL CAN CONNECT WITH ONE ANOTHER INSTEAD OF INDIVIDUALIZING OUR TALENT AND SKILL BECAUSE YOU HAVE TO REMEMBER TO A BIG COMPONENT OF THIS IS THE AUDIENCE IS THE LISTENER IS THE PERSON THAT IS CONSUMING THE ART AND APPRECIATING THE ART AND IT'S A CYCLE RIGHT IT'S IT'S TWO WAY. IT'S A TWO-WAY FLOW RIGHT WE AS THE CRATERS OR ARTIST ARE FEEDING SOMETHING AND PROVIDING A A STORY TO THE LISTENERS TO THE AUDIENCE, AND THE AUDIENCE IS THEN KIND OF TRANSMUTING THAT ART APPRECIATING THAT ART AND FEEDING US AS THE CREATORS AS THE ARTIST.

**Where does the name Luscious Honey come from, and what does it hold that a plainer name would miss?**

Oh honey oh Luscious Honey that name has been around since the pandemic when clubhouse was an audio app where we can go into this world where we can touch each other with our voices, and it was then that I truly discovered how appreciative my voice was in that space, I did not realize the gift that I was holding in and the instrument that my voice is and did not know how to use it. I know I wanted it to. I just didn't know how and so luscious honey was a double Andre of not just how smooth and silky I was with how I look and appear but also and how I spoke and how I used my voice to soothe people and in that created meditations created stretches, created a bedtime story because people were unable to sleep at night and having to be able to have a spaces where I could go into rooms and engage with people using my voice help to build up the confidence to continue to use it because I'm more of a observer really an introvert and I only really speak unless I need to

**Whose hands shaped you before this — a mentor, a family trade, a place? What did they teach you about making things well?**

I would say it's my grandmother. She really knew how to be hospitable and and everything she did. She did it with Care did it with intention, good intentions and really showed people how big our heart was not by what she said, but her actions and she became that Rock that person that central that nucleus for a lot of people and it absolutely was a trait that I wanted to 100% embody I would also say she was also very creative and I've always been around. Very talented people.

**Say plainly, in a sentence or two, why the Luscious Honey Collective needs to exist.**

This needs to exist because we are stronger and we are more force together collectively than we are individually and it's important that we have a space where we can come together and bounce off ideas and also appreciate each other's art

**What was missing in the world that the House is meant to answer?**

What is missing? Is someone who understands not just how to be a creative artist but also someone who understands operations and good business practices because a lot of times creative maybe be missing that and have to go outside of themselves in order to find that to help them grow to help them stretch to help them scale.

**Which of these come closest to what the House is for?**

To make a place rather than a feed, To preserve what would otherwise be lost, Other: A corner I don't wanna say central but a corner where everything is under one roof we're not only can. We attract the resources, but we are the resource.

**If a stranger asked what the House is, in one breath, which is nearest the truth?**

Other: a home for storytellers that spans across various means of media

## The Philosophy

**What belief about creative work will you hold to, whatever it costs?**

That it matters it's not about how good it is. It's about how it makes people feel. It's not about using it as a way to be popular game fame you know have a lot of money, but it feeds and serves a purpose and not just our in the black culture, but in life in general.

**What is the House built to protect? Name the thing you would guard first.**

This collective is built to protect those voices that happen, silenced or under developed, and to create a legacy of storytellers who are liberated and continue on the torch

**When you picture the bar a piece must clear to earn a place here, what does clearing it look like?**

It looks like without hesitation any and everyone would be able to enjoy it

**What does the House refuse to do?**

Chase trends, Flatten voices

**When the work and the audience pull in opposite directions, the House leans toward —**

Whichever the moment asks for

**How does the House stay honest when a room is empty — no event, no new work, nothing to announce?**

I don't really understand this question by staying honest

**What is the most likely way the House could lose its soul, and how will you catch it early?**

When it feels that you are in competition with other creative and artist in the collective or in the house before that will happen on how I will be able to catch. It is to either decide and help them decide whether or not this is still the place for them.

**Where does money belong in the House, and where must it never reach?**

I don't understand this question. Every artist should be paid for their craft or skill and compensated reasonably, and doesn't always have to be with Money specifically, but to be sponsored to be uplifted in highlighted publicly is also a form of compensation.

## The Community

The collective is truly for someone who doesn't have anywhere to put a repository to put their talent or and create either creating the talent and having a workspace that is conducive to creating, and it's also for someone who may need to be written re-energized in some kind before what their day like is before they find me is chaotic and not having a direction

Anyone willing to slow down, People the mainstream overlooks

**The community is at its best when people are —**

Honest without cruelty, Patient with unfinished work, Curious across differences

## The Legacy

**Twenty years from now, what has the House kept that everyone else abandoned?**

i don't know that we have anything abandoned, but what would be left is the early reminants of the version of where they were at a moment in time

**What kind of work deserves to outlive you — and how will the House make room for it?**

early ideas that are no longer musings.. but are fully executed plans

**When someone describes the House to a stranger long after you are gone, what do you hope they say?**

I hope that they say that this was not anything that they knew that they needed but was glad existed

**What did you inherit — from a person, a place, a tradition — that you want the House to pass on?**

Confidence, endurance, patient kindness, and Community

**You will know the House succeeded when —**

A creator does their best work here

**If the House could leave one sentence to the people who come after, what would it be?**

Protected it  at all cost
