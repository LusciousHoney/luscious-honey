# Founder Voice Profile — canonical reference

Private. The living reference for any future writing *in the founder's voice*
(human or AI). Derived from the Founder Interview packet (`founders-note-v1.md`).
Part of Editorial Memory. **Update as new interviews land; never publish this file.**

Public identity is **Luscious Honey / "L.H."** — never a legal name in anything
written from this profile.

## Recurring metaphors & imagery

- **The house / roof / corner / repository** — one place that holds everything.
- **Honey & the voice** — smooth, soothing, "touch each other with our voices"; the
  voice as a gift and instrument.
- **Feeding / the two-way flow** — creator and audience nourish each other; art as a
  cycle, "transmuted" by the listener.
- **The torch** — legacy carried forward by liberated storytellers.
- **The nucleus / Rock / center** — the host who holds people together (grandmother).
- **The amusement park / vacation** — arrival as abundant, joyful over-choice.
- Use these; don't invent competing metaphors (no "platform," "engine," "funnel").

## Favorite sentence structures

- **Additive run-ons** joined by "and… and" — momentum over economy.
- **"It's not about X. It's about Y."** — her core rhetorical move (feeling > polish).
- **Self-correction in the open** — "I don't wanna say central, but a corner…"; keep
  a little of this honesty rather than sanding it perfectly smooth.
- **Warm checit-ins** — "right?"; direct address to a single listener.
- **Virtue lists** — "Confidence, endurance, patient kindness, and Community."
- **Reaching for the ineffable, then naming the feeling** — "a match made in heaven,"
  "absolutely magical."

## Emotional cadence

Opens warm and intimate → **builds and accumulates** → reaches for a feeling she
can't quite name → **lands on it plainly**. Generous, unhurried, occasionally
uncertain and unashamed of it. Writing should breathe the same way: begin close,
gather, resolve on a felt, plain line.

## Leadership philosophy

Lead as **host and nucleus**, not boss. Make separate, personal connections, then
form a "unit." Care and intention shown through **actions, not announcements**.
**Non-competitive** — success is shared; if the House isn't someone's place, help
them see it kindly. "Protect it at all cost."

## Hospitality philosophy

Care with intention (the grandmother). Make arrival feel like a "match made in
heaven" and an amusement park of possibility. **Never require perfection**; never
ask anyone to compromise their ethics or values. Soothe — the House can hold a
tired person.

## Creative philosophy

Art is **reciprocal**; the audience completes it. **Feeling over craft-flexing** —
"how it makes people feel." Center **silenced and underdeveloped voices**. Make
room for **exploration, sensuality, and the body** without shame. **No idea is
impossible.**

## Operational philosophy

Creativity **and** business under one roof — "we are the resource." Help artists
"grow, stretch, scale." Compensate everyone fairly, and know compensation is broad:
pay, **sponsorship, public uplift**. Preserve early versions; turn musings into
"fully executed plans."

## Recurring vocabulary (use)

collective · together · community · voice · resource · honey · torch · liberated ·
silenced · care · intention · feeling · roof / corner / home · uplift · sponsor ·
explore · slow down · patient · endurance · magical · dynamic · "we."

## Words / tone to avoid

- **"Impossible"** (about any idea) — forbidden.
- **Cold institutional / corporate register** — "leverage," "synergy," "content
  engine," "users," "KPIs," "onboarding," "brand activation."
- **Shame or squeamishness** around sensuality, kink, the body, exploration.
- **Competitive / scarcity framing** ("beat," "dominate," "the only," "vs.").
- **Craft-snobbery** ("elevated," "curated excellence") that contradicts feeling-first.
- Over-polishing her voice into flat marketing copy — keep some spoken texture.

## Tone spectrum

- Warm & intimate ⟷ exacting & protective *(she self-selected "Plainspoken, Warm,
  Exacting")*
- Lyrical & soothing ⟷ plainspoken & direct
- Playful & abundant ⟷ solemn & guarding ("protect it at all cost")
Aim for the **center-warm** by default; move toward *protective/solemn* for legacy
and values, toward *playful/abundant* for arrival and invitation.

## Contradictions that make the voice authentic (keep, don't resolve)

- **Open door ⟷ a real bar** (everyone can enjoy it, yet it must earn a place).
- **Curated unit ⟷ ever-widening collective.**
- **Anti-fame ⟷ fierce about fair pay/dignity.**
- **Soft host ⟷ "protect it at all cost."**
These tensions are the texture. Writing that flattens them will sound fake.

## Editorial principles to keep consistent

1. Lead with **people and feeling**, never metrics or craft-flexing.
2. Speak as **"we"**; warmth, care, intention.
3. **Protect and center** overlooked voices.
4. **Never call an idea impossible; never shame exploration.**
5. **Dignity + fair compensation** for every artist, broadly defined.
6. **Cooperative, not competitive.**
7. Keep her **spoken, additive, honest cadence** — resist corporatizing.
8. Honor the **honey / voice / home / torch** imagery.

## Confidence & maintenance

High confidence on: collective ethos, hospitality, reciprocity, protection/legacy,
feeling-over-polish, voice-as-gift. Lower confidence (thin data): the personified
voice, the reciprocal *obligations*, operational specifics, the sensuality value.
Fill these from the recommended follow-ups (see Voice-Analysis.md) before writing
anything that leans on them.
