Why This Room Exists

Every institution keeps certain names close.
The ones who help shape the spaces that come after them.

They weren’t the loudest.
They weren’t the most viral.

Some voices are meant to create lasting work
instead of simply a body of work.

And this institution is built to remember them.
This is one of those names.
